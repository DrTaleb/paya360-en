Automation of Greenhouse intelligent Control System

In order to control the vital factors of the greenhouse, there is no need for a person to have a lot of time of day and night in order to do things and controls manually, resulting in human errors lowering the percentage of productivity and profitability.

Nowadays, greenhouse smartening has attracted the attention of greenhouse owners. Reducing human error and increasing productivity as well as being economically affordable are just some of the benefits of greenhouse smartening.

In a smart greenhouse, all important parameters such as humidity control, temperature, carbon dioxide content, carbon monoxide, wind meter, heater control, heaters, ventilation and any other electrical device, control of pumps and solenoid valves, feedback from flutters (for a smart irrigation) can be controlled remotely.

All of this is only possible by setting up a smart device and installing the relevant remote-control system in your mobile phone.

By smartening the greenhouse, the following can be done:

  • Ventilation control.
  • Control the amount of exposure.
  • Temperature and humidity control (fine-tuned in terms of temperature and humidity).
  • Regular and accurate irrigation.
  • Ensure that heaters, heaters, coolers and any other electrical device work properly.
  • Smart irrigation.
  • Notice of power outages.
  • Immediate information on all important environmental parameters, such as temperature, humidity, carbon dioxide, thermocouple and wind meter, etc.
  • Automatic adjustment or manual control through the application.
  • Hear the ambient sound.

Introducing the features of Paya 360 smart device for industrial greenhouses:

Each paya360 has 8 output connectors (analog and digital), which are used to connect to electrical devices, such as heaters, humidifiers, heaters, fans, coolers, and other electrical equipment, as well as 8 input connectors (analog and digital), which, in addition to detecting the entry and exit of people in the hall, can connect to 4 accurate sensors, temperature and temperature - humidity.

Note: The number of inputs and outputs, as well as the number of sensors, can be increased according to your needs, and given that this device can connect to the network, you can control the number of inputs, outputs and sensors.

  • input: Sensors to detect the presence of humans or to open and close doors and windows, and to detect smoke and fire and any electrical device that needs to be feedback from it, such as a flutter.
  • Output: In order to connect to electrical devices such as heaters, heaters, coolers, water pumps, solenoid valves, etc.
  • Sensor: Temperature and temperature sensors - Humidity with high measurement accuracy as well as carbon dioxide and thermocouple sensors and ...

note: With this device, you can control sensors from any distance, unlike similar devices, there will be no errors in reading sensors by increasing the distance. It can communicate with the app installed on your phone on three communication platforms, which is one of its main advantages.

Other features of Paya 360 remote control device:

  • Graphical and functional system
  • Up to ten users can install the system on their mobile phone, one of them must be the main admin that can add or remove other users, only the main admin can access all pages of the system.
  • All instant reports and any way you define (e.g., only one message, call, activate the siren, etc.) will be sent to you.
  • The device has an e-time and the schedule you define in the system is exactly the same as the device.
  • It has built-in interlock (to keep all engines safe).
  • Switching through the system (for start-stop keys).
  • Time settings for remote control of electrical appliances both minute and second.
  • Announce power outages.
  • After installing the device and attaching the sensors to it, you can define some limits in the system for each sensor individually (e.g., the maximum temperature or humidity limit, as well as the minimum temperature or humidity limit). If the sensor detects a value less or more than the specified limits, then the device automatically turns on or off the desired electric device(s), to keep the temperature and humidity within the limits defined for the environment.
  • You can make greenhouse irrigation quite smart and adjust the hours needed for irrigation on a daily and weekly basis.
  • You will be notified of the correct operation of all electrical appliances and in case of problems with the operation of the electrical appliances you want, you will be notified via SMS or call application and even by turning on the siren on the site.
  • In case of strong winds, the valves should be closed, this operation can be done easily using the wind meter sensor attached to the device.
  • The device is designed in such a way that it can also perform switching, for example the start and stop key can be compressed with this device.
  • It has a built-in interlock, allowing any electric motor to be controlled safely and prevent burns (inner locks for electrical appliances such as coolers, heaters, etc.).
  • If the power goes out, you'll be notified, and then you can determine if it will continue again after the power is disconnected and reconnected while doing one.
  • In addition to daily, weekly and monthly settings, you have the possibility to turn electrical appliances on and off manually and completely graphically through the system installed on your mobile phone.
  • All reports of each sensor are sent to you continuously and are visible in the system, so you can define new settings for the device at any time.
