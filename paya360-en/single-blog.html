Why did we create navok?

Why did we create navok? Navok was formed in 2009 as a result of collective thinking of private sector capitalists and industrialists with entrepreneurs and startup ecosystem activists in Mashhad. This group is trying to support the start-up businesses of Iran's startup ecosystem by establishing active triangular communication between idea owners, investors and experienced consultants.
